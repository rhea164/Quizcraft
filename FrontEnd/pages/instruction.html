For Students

  • 1. Students are required to register themselves on the website.
  • 2. After registration, students can log in to the website using their credentials.
  • 3. Students can view the list of quizzes available to them.
  • 4. Students can attempt the quiz and submit their answers.
  • 5. Students can view their scores after submitting the quiz.
  • 6. Students can view the leaderboard to see the scores of other students.
  • 7. Students can view their profile and update their details.
  • 8. Students can log out from the website after completing the quiz.

For Mentors

  • 1. Mentors are required to register themselves on the website.
  • 2. After registration, mentors can log in to the website using their credentials.
  • 3. Mentors can view the list of quizzes created by them.
  • 4. Mentors can create a new quiz by providing the quiz details and questions.
  • 5. Mentors can view the list of students who have attempted the quiz.
  • 6. Mentors can view the scores of students who have attempted the quiz.
  • 7. Mentors can view their profile and update their details.
  • 8. Mentors can log out from the website after completing the quiz.
