For Students

  • Sign Up: Register on the website by filling in your details to get started.
  • Take a Quiz: Select a quiz to participate in, and follow the instructions:
    • Enter Code: Input the unique quiz code provided by your mentor.
    • Enter Username: Use your username to identify yourself.
  • View Results: Instantly check your score and review feedback on each answer after completing the quiz.
  • Log Out: Securely log out of your account when you're done.
